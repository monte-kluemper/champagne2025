Sanger Voyage 360 — Brut

Crafted by Champagne Sanger in Avize, at the heart of the Côte des Blancs, Voyage 360 is a tribute to the region’s diversity: a blend of grapes from 42 villages that showcase the full richness of Champagne’s terroir.

  • Brut Nature — authentic, no dosage, terroir‑driven.
  • Grapes from 42 villages for a true “360° terroir”.
  • Traditional method with aging on lees.
  • Every purchase supports high‑impact Rotary initiatives.

Behind this Champagne lies a unique cooperative and educational story: Champagne Sanger is led by alumni of the Avize School of Viticulture and Oenology — every bottle celebrates knowledge, sustainability, and craftsmanship.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the money go?

Funds support Rotary Club Madrid International projects and Rotary International causes throughout the year. This year we’ve partnered with other clubs on projects such as Fuentes para Belén (Israel) and Employing Rotary (Spain), and collaborate locally with NGOs like the Centro de Acogida Catalina Labouré in Madrid and Pueblos con Futuro which is active across a number of Spanish provinces.

What’s the technical profile of Sanger Voyage 360?

A Brut Nature with no dosage after disgorgement, showcasing purity and precision. Sourced from a blend of 42 villages across Champagne: Chardonnay (50%), Pinot Noir (30%), and Pinot Meunier (20%). ABV: ~12% vol. Pressure: ~6 bar. Traditional method (secondary fermentation in bottle).

How does it taste?

Fresh, mineral nose with citrus, green apple and white flowers. On the palate it’s tense, elegant and dry with fine bubbles and a long finish of toasted almond and brioche. The no‑dosage style highlights the terroir’s authenticity.

Serving & pairings?

Serve at 7–9 °C, ideally in a white‑wine glass to capture its aromas. Perfect with oysters, sushi, soft cheeses, white fish or savoury appetizers. Also ideal as a festive welcome wine.

Awards & ratings?

Sanger Voyage 360 has earned international recognition for its precision, balance, and authentic expression of the Champagne terroir. Among its distinctions:

  • Gold Medal — Gilbert & Gaillard International Challenge
  • Silver Medal — Concours Mondial de Bruxelles
  • 90+ points — Wine Enthusiast magazine
  • Recommended Champagne — Le Guide Hachette des Vins

These awards highlight the excellence of a Brut Nature that combines technical mastery with genuine character — a Champagne appreciated by professionals and wine lovers alike.
